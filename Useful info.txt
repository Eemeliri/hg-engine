Useful stuff to know:
- If you are using MelonDS and your dpad does not work in battle, disable "Literal optimisations" in MelondS Settings->CPU Emulation

- Goldenrod sells mints and ability capsules
- Buenas radio show rewards better items such as ability patches when reaching certain point milestones
- Ruins of Alph backrooms have greater rewards
- Union Cave post-surf is full of strong trainers, but the rewards may be worth it
- Several item balls have been edited to contain new items
- Many species learn an attacking move slightly earlier to reduce the tedium of having to use pound/tackle/scratch for too many levels
- Non-berry held items are restored after battle
